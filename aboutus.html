ABOUT US

Our Story

Fxdcord is a business made up of young and creative individuals passionate about crafting handmade fashion accessories designed for a youthful audience. At just twenty (20) years old, I began my entrepreneurial journey with the support of friends and family. Within a few months, my business started to flourish and expand, particularly in the online space.

The brand's products radiate a beach vibe, incorporating real seashells into our designs. The bracelets, anklets, and necklaces we create have become trendy and highly sought after among young people today.

Began

The business began on June 9, 2022, in Paracale, Camarines Norte, with the hope of gaining recognition not only within the province but throughout the Philippines. We now have multiple resellers and business partners using the same brand name across the country.

As young entrepreneurs in the industry, we have faced several challenges, one of which is establishing a loyal customer base. During our initial months, it was difficult to attract and retain customers since we were selling our products primarily on the streets of Paracale and Jose Panganiban in Camarines Norte. This challenge was compounded by the fact that the market is already saturated with established brands and that both Paracale and Jose Panganiban are traditionally known for producing fine jewelry rather than our type of fashion accessories.

To overcome this, we focused our efforts on marketing our products online to reach a wider and younger audience.

We Found

Last year, while my brother and I were selling our handmade products on the streets of Paracale, I was approached by the Negosyo Center Business Counselor of the municipality. She encouraged me to register my business name and offered to invite me to join a local trade fair at SM City Daet. Since then, I have participated in numerous local trade fairs in the province, including regional trade fairs.

In May, we took part in the Orgullo Kan Bikol (OKB) event at Shangri-La Plaza in Mandaluyong City. The LGU Paracale supported us, and we participated again this October in the National Arts and Crafts Fair at SM Megamall in Mandaluyong. With the assistance of the NCBC, who helped subsidize my registration and fare worth Php 3,500.00, we were able to attend.

Additionally, I participated in two Product Development activities organized by the DTI. This year, I will join the Kapatid Mentor ME program to further expand my knowledge in managing my business through mentoring.

Advocacy

FxdCord advocates for brotherhood, freedom, the arts, and fixed-gear culture. Our core belief is to stay focused and disciplined, setting clear goals and working consistently toward achieving them. We also encourage taking risks and viewing failure as a stepping stone toward success.

Moreover, we emphasize the importance of surrounding yourself with a strong support system, including mentors like DTI staff, advisors, and peers who can provide valuable guidance and feedback. Finally, we believe in continuous learning and adapting to new ideas and technologies to stay ahead in the industry and improve our craft.

Own

FxdCord is owned and created by a young man named April Atanacio Jr., who lives in Paracale, Camarines Norte. Growing up in a broken family, he had little but a dream to become a fine artist. Throughout his teenage years, he developed a passion for fixed-gear cycling, mural painting, and creating various forms of art such as portraits, landscapes, charcoal drawings, and visual arts. Anything related to art and crafts captured his interest. From a young age, his mother supported and nurtured his passion for art.

At the age of 19, April made a promise to himself that when he turned 20, he would no longer be a teenager. He believed that from that point on, he would no longer rely on others for support. His philosophy was simple: whatever you do with your heart, money will follow.

Two months before his 20th birthday in May, while he was in Manila, April began to form the idea for his business. He eventually decided to leave school to focus on this vision. On June 9, 2022, FxdCord officially came to life, blending his love for bikes and art into accessories that would leave a lasting impact across generations. By July 9, 2022, his 20th birthday, FxdCord had turned from an idea into a reality. This is the story of how FxdCord was created.
